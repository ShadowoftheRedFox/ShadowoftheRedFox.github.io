Why mini games?

I made mini games mostly for fun, and to try different kind of method that I might need to use in the future. Those mini games are just exemples I made width.

Here is some informations about those:

Asteroid:

You're a spaceship, and your goal is to reach the highest score you can.
Even is the gameplay is lame, it was fun developing it:
- The asteroid forms are generated with maths using a circle and variatiobs.
- They rotate using a matrice.
- The move following a vector.
- An finally, the collision with the player are exact using triangles (lots of them).

Controls are:
- qd/ad/←→.
- z/w/↑ to accelerate.
- s/↓ to decelerate.
- Space/x to shoot.

I tried to make it playable on phone, it's not perfect but I'm proud of the system that came off of it.

Each white bullet deal 1 damage, red ones deal 2 damages. You get another bullet each two levels, and you get a level each 200 points. You get one more life each 5000 points. Speeding up increase the score faster.
